Okay, so here's a sentence I didn't think I'd be writing in 2026: even RuneScape is getting pulled into the great September stampede. RuneScape: Dragonwilds is officially getting its 1.0 launch on PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X and S on September 15, 2026, which also makes it the very first time a RuneScape title has shown up on a console. The game has been sitting in early access on PC for a while now, so anyone who's been waiting for the polished version to land everywhere at once finally has a date circled.
