Okay, the weekend is officially here, and honestly? My wallet is already nervous. IGN just dropped their roundup of the best deals for Saturday, June 20, and a couple of them are tough to ignore if you've been sitting on the fence.

Let's start with the Nintendo pick. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom — Switch 2 Edition — is down to $59.99 this weekend, which saves you $20 off the usual price. You get the physical copy with the paid upgrade, so performance enhancements and Zelda Notes come bundled in. The cool part is the game works on both the original Switch and the Switch 2, so nobody's locked out. Not a bad deal for one of the strongest games Nintendo's put out in years.

Then there's AirPods Pro 3 sitting at $169. If you've been waiting to upgrade from the 2nd gen, this one's worth a look — they bring a built-in heart rate monitor, which is a genuinely nice addition for anyone who uses their earbuds during workouts. AirPods don't go on sale often enough to sleep on, and $169 is a reasonable ask.

The rest of the roundup is a solid mix for gamers. Nioh 3 made the list, alongside Donkey Kong Country Returns HD and Splatoon Raiders, so there's something for the action-RPG crowd and anyone looking for something more colorful and chaotic.

Between Tears of the Kingdom and those AirPods, June 20 is shaping up to be a dangerous day for my budget. If you've been holding out for a Switch 2 pickup or a new pair of buds, this might be your weekend.
