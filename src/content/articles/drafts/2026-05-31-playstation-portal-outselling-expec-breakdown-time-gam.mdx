Sony baru je bagitahu yang PlayStation Portal resale hot gila la. Diorang cakap device ni jual lagi power dari apa yang diorang expect,mmg tak sangka la. 

Portal ni basically remote play device untuk PS5, senang cerita korang boleh main game dekat mana-mana guna skrin dia. Remote play existed before but Portal ni buat semua orang teruja because dia ada screen sendiri, controller sendiri, and most importantly, confirm confirm Sony buat la.  

Tapi legit la, siapa sangka device yang ramai cakap "alah ni just remote play je" boleh jual macam tu? Sony surprised juga kot. Diorang kata the demand was unexpected, which means diorang tak expect orang melampau sangat nak beli. 

Apa yang best Pasal Portal ni is that dia Bagi yang duduk dalam transit or travel selalu, this thing is perfect. Takyah nak bring whole console or nak rely on phone/ipad. Setup dia pun senang gila, connect with your PS5 and done. 

Tapi weh, ada satu part yang annoying sikit - Portal only works dengan PS5 je, so kalau korang still main PS4, tak boleh use la. And also need stable WiFi untuk main smooth, tak boleh main offline.

Still, looks like Sony menang kali ni dengan device ni. Diorang jarang salah estimate sales, but this one exceeded expectations for sure. 

Maybe this signals satu trend baru where gaming devices more focused on portability and convenience? Could be big thing for future la. 

We'll see what happens next, but for now Sony can celebrate sikit la.
