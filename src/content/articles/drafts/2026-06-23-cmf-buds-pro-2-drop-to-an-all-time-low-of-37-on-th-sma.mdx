Okay so I don't usually get excited about budget earbuds, but $37 for the CMF Buds Pro 2? That's the kind of number that makes you do a double take. We're talking about an all-time low on Amazon, dropping right before Prime Day even officially kicks off, which means either someone jumped the gun on the sales or these things were about to be everywhere on deal pages anyway.

Here's the thing that gets me though: these aren't just cheap earbuds coasting on a low price. The CMF Buds Pro 2 pack in features that would've sounded like wishlist items a couple of years ago. You've got up to 50dB of hybrid active noise cancellation, dual drivers for actual decent sound separation, and a case with a customizable Smart Dial that lets you tweak controls on the fly. That last bit is genuinely clever — most budget cases are just... cases. This one has a little personality.

The original pitch on these was already strong for the price, and now we're looking at a sub-$40 price tag on a pair that genuinely competes with stuff well above that. If you've been holding out on grabbing new earbuds because the good ones felt like too much of a splurge, this is the moment. Amazon has the deal live right now, and I'd expect stock to get a little weird as Prime Day officially rolls around.

Honestly, deals like this are why I keep a wishlist going year-round. You see a product you were curious about, wait for the dip, and suddenly what was a "maybe someday" purchase becomes a no-brainer. The CMF Buds Pro 2 at $37 is a no-brainer. Just don't wait too long, because all-time lows have a funny way of selling out before the day is over.
